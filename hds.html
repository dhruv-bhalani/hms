About AuraHealth Center

Over 25 Years of Medical Trust, Healthcare Quality & Safety

Formed in 2001, AuraHealth Medical Center has evolved into a leading quaternary hospital group. We utilize high-resolution imaging scanners, ultra-modern robotic-assisted surgical systems, and state-of-the-art diagnostic medical laboratories to offer precise therapeutics.
